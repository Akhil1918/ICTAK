The Way of Kings

Brandon Sanderson

About the Book

The Way of Kings is the first book in the Stormlight Archive series. It introduces a richly detailed world ravaged by tempests, where wars are fought on the Shattered Plains. The story explores the lives of a surgeon-turned-soldier, a bright-eyed scholar, and a mysterious assassin bound by conflicting loyalties.

Book Details

  • Genre: Fantasy
  • Published: 2010
  • Pages: 1007
  • Awards: David Gemmell Legend Award

Neuromancer

William Gibson

About the Book

Neuromancer is a seminal work of cyberpunk science fiction that revolutionized the genre. The novel follows Case, a washed-up computer hacker hired by a mysterious employer for one last job. Set in a dystopian future where technology and humanity are deeply intertwined, the story explores themes of artificial intelligence, corporate power, and the blurred lines between human consciousness and digital reality.

Book Details

  • Genre: Science Fiction
  • Published: 1984
  • Pages: 271
  • Awards: Nebula Award, Philip K. Dick Award, Hugo Award
